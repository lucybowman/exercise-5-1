Frontend Practice Templates

Pencil Sketch Wireframe

Style Observations

  • Image: Images placed as cards in rows of 4 as thumbnails for each project template
  • Palette: #000000, #ffffff, #cafc9f, #f9d455, #fdfe88, #f8ebd9
  • Text: Serif and Sans Serif fonts, first line weight is heaviest and then decreases moving down, about 3 sizes, some use of italic, center alignment
  • Header: Fixed, centered position at top
  • Footer: Fixed, centered position at bottom
  • Navigation Centered, fixed position. Secondary nav links at bottom and top right
